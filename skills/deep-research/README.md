# Deep Research

Multi-source research using firecrawl and exa MCPs for thorough, cited reports.

## What This Skill Does

Performs web research across multiple sources, synthesizes findings, and produces cited reports with source attribution.

## When It Activates

- User asks to research any topic in depth
- Competitive analysis, technology evaluation, or market sizing
- Due diligence on companies, investors, or technologies
- Questions requiring synthesis from multiple sources

## Requirements

- Requires firecrawl and/or exa MCP servers
- Both together give best coverage

## Key Topics Covered

- Multi-source search strategy (15-30 unique sources)
- Deep-reading key sources
- Report synthesis with citations
- Parallel research with subagents
- Quality rules (every claim needs a source, cross-reference, acknowledge gaps)
