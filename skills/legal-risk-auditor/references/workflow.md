# Legal Risk Review Workflow

**Load this reference when:** conducting contract risk reviews.

## 4-Step Workflow

### Step 1: Fact Extraction

Extract from contract:
- Contract type (买卖、租赁、服务、劳动)
- Parties (自然人、法人、非法人组织)
- Subject matter (goods, services, property, IP)
- Rights and obligations
- Timeframes and deadlines
- Breach consequences
- Dispute resolution mechanism

### Step 2: Legal Basis

Retrieve relevant provisions:
- Mandatory vs discretionary provisions
- Violation consequences
- Burden of proof assignment

### Step 3: Risk Assessment

Evaluate across three dimensions:
- Severity (Critical/Medium/Low)
- Probability (High/Medium/Low)
- Fixability (Easy/Medium/Hard)

### Step 4: Report Generation

Produce structured report with:
- Risk labels (🔴🟡🔵)
- Legal references
- Modification suggestions

## Risk Labels

| Label | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 🔴 高危 | Contract invalidity risk | Must modify |
| 🟡 中危 | Performance dispute risk | Negotiate |
| 🔵 优化 | Precision improvement | Suggest |
